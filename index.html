About me

Lingyan Ran received his Bachelor degree and Ph.D. degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), Xi'an, China, in 2011 and 2018. Earlier, he was a visiting scholar in Stevens Institute of Technology (SIT), Hoboken, NJ, from 2013 to 2015. His research interests include image classification and semantic segmentation. He is currently a member of China Computer Federation (CCF) , China Society of Image and Graphics (CSIG).
